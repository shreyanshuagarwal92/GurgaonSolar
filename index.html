Frequently asked questions

What should be the size of my installation?

Size of a system depends on a lot of factors including your building's load profile, sanctioned load, type of system, etc. Don't worry, we will come by, assess everything and recommend the most optimum system size for you.

What are the different types of system?

There are essentially three types of systems: grid-tied, off-grid and hybrid (download this document to understand which type of system suits you best).

Is subsidy available? Should I wait for subsidy?

Yes. A capital subsidy is available for residential and not-for-profit institutional customers. Subsidy is not available for any commercial or industrial building. Apply for subsidy here: capital subsidy application form

How to apply for subsidy?

We will apply for subsidy on your behalf. Subsidy is available through HAREDA and SECI in Gurgaon.

How to apply for net-metering?

We will apply for net-metering on your behalf. Gurgaon's Discom is expected to start providing approvals for net-metering soon. You can download the application form for net-metering here

How many plants has Gurgaon Solar installed in the city?

We are the largest rooftop solar installation company in Gurgaon and have installed over 50 rooftop solar plants in Gurugram alone. Some our our installations in Gurgaon are located in Sushant Lok, DLF Phase 1 & 2, Palam Vihar, Sector 45, 46 & 47, South City 1, Nirvana Country, etc. Apart from this, the parent company, Loop Solar, is one of the largest distributors of Tier I solar modules and inverters in India.

Which module brands are preffered for subsidy based projects?

As subsidy based projects need to use 'Made in India' modules, we prefer to use - Adani Solar, Vikram Solar and Waaree Solar.
